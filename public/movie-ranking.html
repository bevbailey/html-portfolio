The Best Movies According to Bev

My top 3 movies of 2023.

The Harder They Fall

Modern day western. Dramatic, funny and cool scenes. Some characters based on real life people.

Creed 3

We follow Adonis as he faces new problems. Dealing with family, friends and fame on another level.

Talk To Me

A24 studio just gets it. This movie had me thinking about it for weeks after. Scary and psych thriller rolled into one.
